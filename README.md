# About Me
**Vibe Coder | Problem Creator | Solution Finder**

Welcome to my corner of GitHub! Ignore the username—it seemed like a good idea at the time, but we’re all living with our past commits, right?

I’m a developer who thrives in the space between "why is this breaking?" and "wait, it actually works." I categorize my work into two modes:

Standard Coding: Building structured, functional software.

Vibe Coding: Deep-diving into experimental logic where I learn the most by breaking things spectacularly.

---

## Current Focus: Financial AI Insights
I’m currently building an AI-driven financial services platform. The goal is to democratize investment data by shifting the focus from "expert" opinions to autonomous trend analysis.

Status: Private Beta (Moving to Public soon!)

Mission: Empowering investors to rely on historical data and AI insights rather than external noise.

Tech Stack: Python, Machine Learning frameworks, and a lot of caffeine.
